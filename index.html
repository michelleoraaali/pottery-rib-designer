Pottery Rib Customizer

Make your own custom pottery rib! Play around with the different features to generate a 3D model. If you want to 3D print the model, choose .STL or .OBJ when downloading. If you prefer to use a lasercutter to create your ribs, choose .SVG, but keep in mind that you will have to sand or shape the rib to include the bevel you desire!
