3D Printing Tips
Recommended Settings:
  • Layer Height: 0.2mm for standard quality, 0.1mm for smooth finish
  • Infill: 20-30% (higher for rigid ribs, lower for flexible)
  • Supports: Usually not needed
  • Print Orientation: Flat on build plate (unless you've added longitudinal curve)
Material Recommendations:
  • PLA: Best for rigid ribs, easy to print
  • PETG: More durable, semi-flexible
  • TPU: For flexible ribs (adjust thickness accordingly)
Important Notes:
  • Edge thickness below 0.5mm may require fine nozzle (0.3mm or smaller)
  • Ensure your slicer respects minimum wall thickness
  • Test with small models before full-size prints
  • Post-process edges with sandpaper for smoothest finish
